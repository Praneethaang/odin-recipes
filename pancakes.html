Pancakes

Description

Fluffy pancakes that are golden on the outside and soft inside, perfect for breakfast with syrup and butter.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tbsp melted butter

Steps

  1. In a bowl, whisk together flour, sugar, and baking powder.
  2. In another bowl, mix egg, milk, and melted butter.
  3. Combine wet and dry ingredients until just mixed.
  4. Heat a pan and pour batter to form pancakes.
  5. Cook until bubbles form, flip, and cook until golden.
