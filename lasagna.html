Lasagna

Description

This lasagna is layered with rich meat sauce, creamy ricotta, and gooey mozzarella. It’s the ultimate comfort food for family dinners.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Garlic & onions

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook noodles according to package instructions.
  3. Prepare meat sauce with ground beef, tomato sauce, garlic, and onions.
  4. Layer noodles, sauce, ricotta, and mozzarella in a baking dish.
  5. Repeat layers, top with Parmesan.
  6. Bake for 45 minutes, let cool before serving.
