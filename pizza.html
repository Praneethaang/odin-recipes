Pizza

Description

A homemade pizza with a crispy crust, tangy tomato sauce, and melty mozzarella that makes every bite pure happiness.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Olive oil
  • Fresh basil
  • Toppings of choice (pepperoni, mushrooms, etc.)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface.
  3. Spread tomato sauce evenly over the dough.
  4. Top with cheese and your favorite toppings.
  5. Bake for 12–15 minutes until crust is golden.
